origins:

After witnessing the murder of my parents Dr. Thomas Wayne and Martha Wayne as a child, I swore vengeance against criminals, an oath tempered by a sense of justice. I trained myself to physical and intellectual perfection and crafted a bat-inspired persona to fight crime.Unlike most superheroes, I do not possess any superpowers; I make use (to the best that I can) of my intellect, detective skills, science and technology, wealth, physical prowess, and intimidation in his war on crime.
